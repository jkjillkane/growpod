How It Works

Smart Growing, Simplified

Our ESP32-powered system automates every aspect of microgreen cultivation using precision sensors and intelligent control.

01

Plant Your Seeds

Place microgreen seeds on the hydroponic growing medium inside the chamber. No soil needed.

02

Set & Forget

The ESP32 microcontroller takes over — managing grow lights, watering schedules, temperature, and humidity automatically.

03

Monitor Remotely

Real-time sensor data lets you check on CO₂ levels, moisture, and climate conditions from anywhere.

04

Harvest Fresh

In just 7–14 days, harvest nutrient-dense microgreens straight from your countertop.

Features

Everything Your Greens Need

Precision-engineered components working together for optimal growing conditions.

💡

Scheduled Grow Lights

Automated lighting cycles optimised for microgreen growth phases. Full-spectrum LEDs ensure rapid, healthy development.

  • Programmable light schedules
  • Full-spectrum LED
  • Low power consumption
💧

Smart Irrigation

Capacitive soil moisture sensor (SEN0193) monitors growing medium moisture in real-time, triggering peristaltic pumps only when needed.

  • Capacitive sensing (corrosion-resistant)
  • 3.3–5.5V operating range
  • Automatic pump control
🌡

Climate Control

Automated temperature and humidity management maintains the perfect microclimate for consistent growth.

  • Temperature regulation
  • Humidity management
  • Closed-loop control
💨

CO₂ Monitoring

The ENS160 air quality sensor provides precise CO₂ and VOC readings, ensuring optimal growing air conditions.

  • ENS160 digital sensor
  • CO₂ + VOC monitoring
  • Real-time data logging

Hydroponic System

No soil required. Our hydroponic approach uses 90% less water while delivering nutrients directly to plant roots.

  • Soilless growing
  • 90% water savings
  • Faster growth cycles

ESP32 Brain

The ESP32 microcontroller orchestrates all sensors and actuators, with WiFi connectivity for remote monitoring.

  • WiFi enabled

Current Draw 5 mA
Interface PH2.0-3P (Gravity)
Dimensions 98 × 23 mm
Sensing Type Capacitive (non-corrosive)

Capacitive sensing provides longer lifespan than resistive alternatives. The sensor outputs an analog voltage inversely proportional to moisture level, calibrated in three zones: Dry, Wet, and Water.
