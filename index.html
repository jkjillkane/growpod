Light That Adjusts Itself

Your greens get the exact light they need at every growth stage — automatically. Full-spectrum LEDs run on a schedule, so you never have to think about it.

Water Only When Needed

A built-in sensor monitors moisture levels in real time. When your greens are thirsty, the pump kicks in. When they’re not, it doesn’t. Zero waste.

  • Automatic pump control
  • No overwatering, no underwatering

Component Details Est. Cost

Moisture Sensor: SEN0193

Operating Voltage 3.3 – 5.5 VDC
Output Voltage 0 – 3.0 VDC
Current Draw 5 mA
Interface PH2.0-3P (Gravity)
Dimensions 98 × 23 mm
Sensing Type Capacitive (non-corrosive)

Capacitive sensing provides longer lifespan than resistive alternatives. The sensor outputs an analog voltage inversely proportional to moisture level, calibrated in three zones: Dry, Wet, and Water.
